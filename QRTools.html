💡 Generator Tips

Best practices for QR codes:

  • Use high contrast colors for better scanning
  • Test your QR code with multiple devices
  • For URLs, use HTTPS for security
  • Keep the content concise for simpler codes
  • Add a call-to-action near the QR code

Common uses for QR codes:

  • Website URLs
  • Contact information (vCards)
  • Wi-Fi network credentials
  • Payment information
  • Event details
